Final notes

  • They don’t say why using the HACK method makes sense. They had to create and additional HACK doner, so it did not reduce the number of transgenics that needed to be created.

  • They could have explored more of the biology of why the gene drive performed so much worse in a fGD configuration.

  • Didn’t do anything with the fact that the Cas9 insertion can act as a unlinked marker to track homing vs meiotic drive.

  • All models are wrong, but some are useful. In an effort to be less wrong I think they sacrificed most of the usefulness.

  • They hint at trans-generational deposition a few time. I think they need to lay the foundation for this more before speculating about it.
